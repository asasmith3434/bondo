Hockey

One of the oldest sports in the existance of man yet one of the most underrated sports still. Hockey combines many athletic features into one sport; balance, hand eye coordination, timing, and ect. Furthermore, Hockey combines three of the most popular sport concepts created; skating, basketball, and boxing. Figure skaters make careers out of only skating, which is difficult enough, whereas a hockey player has to not only skate but play in another game at the same time. The formation and positioning on the ice mimics the form of a basketball court. Lastly it hockey offers the chance for viewers to experience a boxing match in a different environment. Therefore, my cause to spread the word that no other sport is as entertaining to watch as hockey.
